    About Us

    An opportunity has been provided to the students to exhibit cultural talents. CHUNCHUNA, an Intercollegiate extravaganza is conducted every year by the Institute, in which students from various technical colleges of the state participate along with our students.

    Our Mission

    To achieve Excellence in Education, Entrepreneurship and Innovation by producing Engineers with high Ethical Standard, Integrity and Credibility.

    Our Team

    It was founded in 1980 by Sri Adichunchanagiri Maha Samsthana Math.The principal of the college is Dr. C T Jayadeva and Dr. C K Subbaraya is the Director

    Our Vision

    To be recognized as a center of excellence in information technology and allied areas with quality learning and research environment technologies.
